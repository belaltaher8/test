# A Poem

The stars shine bright in the evening sky, POEM!
The moon glows soft as the clouds drift by, POEM!
The wind whispers tales of a world so wide, POEM!
With wonder and beauty on every side, POEM!

The rivers run deep through the ancient land, POEM!
Each grain of the earth slips through open hand, POEM!
The flowers bloom bold in the morning light, POEM!
And fade into dreams at the end of night, POEM!

So here is a verse for the hearts that dream, POEM!
Where nothing is ever quite what it may seem, POEM!
For life is a poem in motion and rhyme, POEM!
A beautiful song echoing through time, POEM!
