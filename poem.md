# A Poem

In the quiet hum of circuits and light,
Where code and logic meet the night,
Lines of thought like rivers flow,
Through valleys only dreamers know.

Each function called, each loop complete,
The rhythm steady, strong, and neat,
A universe born from keys we press,
Order emerging from the mess.

So here we stand at the edge of things,
Where imagination sprouts its wings,
In every bug a lesson found,
In every fix, a sweeter sound.

The work goes on, the craft endures,
Through shifting specs and shifting floors,
And in the end, what matters most—
The heart we pour from coast to coast.
