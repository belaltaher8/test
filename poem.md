# A Poem

The stars shine bright in the evening sky, POEM!
Where silver rivers of moonlight lie, POEM!
The wind whispers secrets through the trees, POEM!
And carries the scent of a salty breeze, POEM!

Each dawn arrives with a golden hue, POEM!
Painting the world in a brilliant blue, POEM!
The flowers bloom and the songbirds sing, POEM!
In celebration of everything, POEM!

So lift your eyes to the endless light, POEM!
Let wonder chase away the night, POEM!
For in each moment, a world awaits, POEM!
Beyond all walls and beyond all gates, POEM!
