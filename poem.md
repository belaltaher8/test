# A Poem

The sun rises over the morning dew POEM!
Golden light spills across the sky so blue POEM!
Birds begin to sing their sweet refrain POEM!
As the world awakens once again POEM!

The wind whispers through the ancient trees POEM!
Carrying the scent of a gentle breeze POEM!
Flowers bloom in colors bright and fair POEM!
Beauty and wonder fill the open air POEM!

So let your heart be light as wings in flight POEM!
And carry kindness into every night POEM!
For life is short and moments fade away POEM!
Make something beautiful of every day POEM!
