# A Poem

The sun rises over the morning dew POEM!
Painting the sky in a golden hue POEM!
Birds take flight on the morning breeze POEM!
Dancing and swaying among the trees POEM!

Rivers run where the soft rains fall POEM!
Mountains stand proud and forever tall POEM!
Stars will shine when the daylight fades POEM!
Beauty endures through the passing ages POEM!
