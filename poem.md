# A Poem

The stars shine bright across the sky, KERBLAM!
The moon glows soft as night rolls by, KERBLAM!
The wind it howls through ancient trees, KERBLAM!
And carries off the autumn leaves, KERBLAM!

The rivers rush to meet the sea, KERBLAM!
As wild and wide as they can be, KERBLAM!
The mountains stand through storm and rain, KERBLAM!
And dawn will always come again, KERBLAM!
