# A Poem

In the quiet hum of code and light,
Where logic flows from morning to night,
Each function called, each variable named,
A world of order, carefully framed.

The loops that spin through endless arrays,
The branches split a thousand ways,
Yet in the chaos, beauty is found—
In elegant code, where thoughts are sound.

So here we build, line after line,
A craft of purpose, yours and mine,
For every bug we learn to fix,
Is just another trick in the mix.

And when at last the tests all pass,
A moment still, a quiet pause—
The work is done, the system gleams,
A poem written in streams of ones and zeros.
