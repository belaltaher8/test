# A Poem

The morning sun begins to rise,
And paints the gold across the skies,
The birds take flight on gentle wings,
And greet the dawn with songs it brings.

The river flows through valleys wide,
With secrets locked within its tide,
The mountains stand so tall and proud,
Their peaks adorned with drifting cloud.

In every leaf and blade of grass,
In every shadow as clouds pass,
There lives a story yet untold,
A tale of wonder to behold.

So open wide your heart and see,
The beauty all around you, free,
For life is but a fleeting dream,
Far more than what it seems.
