# A Poem

The stars above in quiet skies,
Cast silver light before our eyes.
The wind moves soft through autumn leaves,
And dances free among the trees.

A river hums its gentle song,
And carries all our dreams along.
The mountains stand in patient grace,
Each stone a mark of time and space.

So let us rest in wonder here,
Where sky meets earth and all is clear.
For in this world of endless change,
There's beauty found in every range.
