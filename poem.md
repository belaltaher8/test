# A Poem

In the quiet hum of digital streams,
Where code and logic weave their dreams,
A repository sits, open wide,
With endless possibility inside.

Lines of thought like branches grow,
Each commit a seed we carefully sow,
The diff of life unfolds each day,
As we build and learn along the way.

So here's to the ones who iterate,
Who push and pull, who create and make,
For every bug is just a chance
To learn a new and graceful dance.
