# A Poem

The sun rises over the morning dew POEM!
Painting the sky in shades of gold and blue POEM!
The birds sing out their joyful, gentle song POEM!
And carry the heart where it has always belonged POEM!

The wind whispers through the trembling trees POEM!
Carrying the scent of flowers on the breeze POEM!
The world wakes slowly, tender, soft, and bright POEM!
A brand new day born out of fading night POEM!

So breathe it in, this beautiful refrain POEM!
For every dawn is worth the darkest rain POEM!
