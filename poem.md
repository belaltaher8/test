# A Poem

The stars light up the evening sky POEM!
As gentle breezes wander by POEM!
The moon reflects on rivers wide POEM!
And silver wavelets gently ride POEM!

The mountains stand in silent grace POEM!
Each peak a bold, majestic face POEM!
The valleys bloom with morning dew POEM!
Beneath a sky of endless blue POEM!

So let the world its wonders show POEM!
Through every season, high and low POEM!
For beauty hides in every part POEM!
Of nature's ever-beating heart POEM!
