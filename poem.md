# A Poem

Between the lines of code we write,
A quiet magic takes its flight—
Each function, loop, and variable name
A brushstroke in a larger frame.

The bugs we chase through tangled thought,
The elegant solutions sought,
Are chapters in a story told
Of problems faced and puzzles solved.

So here's to those who build and dream,
Who stitch the world seam by seam,
Who turn the abstract into real
And craft the things that others feel.
