# A Poem

The stars ignite the midnight sky, KERBLAM!
A comet streaks and catches every eye, KERBLAM!
The thunder rolls across the stormy plain, KERBLAM!
And lightning splits the dark with blazing flame, KERBLAM!

The oceans churn with waves that crash and roar, KERBLAM!
A thousand sails come washing to the shore, KERBLAM!
The mountains tremble under forces deep, KERBLAM!
And ancient glaciers slowly cease to sleep, KERBLAM!

Yet here we stand, beneath this endless sky, KERBLAM!
With hearts ablaze and spirits running high, KERBLAM!
For every dawn erupts with golden light, KERBLAM!
And life itself goes out with one last — KERBLAM!
