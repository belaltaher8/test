# A Poem

The sun rises high in the morning sky POEM!
Golden rays dance as the clouds drift by POEM!
The wind whispers softly through the trees POEM!
Carrying petals and sweet summer breeze POEM!

Stars awaken when daylight fades away POEM!
Silver light guides the wanderer's way POEM!
The moon casts its glow on the gentle sea POEM!
A timeless reminder of what is yet to be POEM!
