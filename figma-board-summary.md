# Figma Board Summary: Copilot Extensibility Team Retro (8/20/2025)

## Board Overview
This is a team retrospective board for the Copilot Extensibility Team held on August 20, 2025. The board uses a FigJam template with sticky notes organized into several thematic sections to capture team feedback, concerns, and action items.

## Board Structure

The board contains the following main sections:

1. **Ice Breaker Section** - "If you were an inanimate object, what would you be?" (Team building activity)
2. **Positives** - What went well during this period
3. **Learnings, Questions, Concerns** - Issues and topics requiring discussion
4. **Take Aways** - Action items and next steps
5. **Lacked/Do Less (Negatives)** - Areas for improvement
6. **Quick Tips** - FigJam tutorial section

## Summary

This retrospective reveals a team experiencing positive momentum with improved on-call experiences, strong collaboration through pairing, and successful technical deliveries around Proxima and job queuing. The team is welcoming new members effectively and appreciates stable interim leadership.

However, challenges remain around communication overload (especially Slack noise), clarity on ownership and priorities, work-life balance during intense delivery periods, and ensuring First Responder work receives appropriate recognition alongside feature development.

The team has identified concrete action items focused on better communication tools, refined processes, clearer planning visibility, and sustainable time management practices.
