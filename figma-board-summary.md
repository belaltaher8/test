## ❓ Learnings, Questions & Concerns

- **Support escalations:** Is Tim handling these alone? Should FR help? Do we have bandwidth?
- **Vision clarity:** Improvements noted (planning docs, release issues, demo meetings) — but is more needed?
- **DRI/ownership ambiguity:** Still unclear who owns what, especially with external teams
- **GA readiness:** Does the team have a voice in the GA conversation? What's the expected load?
- **Team offsite:** Likely October, tentatively after Universe launch (TBD)
- **Next brown bag topic** — open for suggestions

---

## 🏆 Take Aways & Action Items

1. **Refine the FR (First Responder) backlog**
2. **Route external requests to public team channel / GitHub issues** instead of DMs
3. **Explore Slack AI summarization** to reduce communication overhead
4. **Assign Copilot to auto-generate docs on our code** *(Cal to drive)*
5. **Revive/build a GitHub Action for FR label automation** (e.g., auto-add `first-responder` label and surface in a board)
6. **Tim to finalize Universe launch schedule** within 1–2 weeks of retro date
7. **Be more deliberate with upfront estimation** to avoid over-commitment
8. **Provide high-level roadmap overviews in larger group meetings**
