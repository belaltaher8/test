## Key Sections

### 1. Positives (What Went Well)

#### Team Dynamics & Collaboration
- **Welcome Dan!** - New team member Dan joined and made a strong first impression with a fast PR fix for gh/gh.
- People are pairing more on epics together this month - team collaboration is increasing.
- Mentoring Dan has been a lot of fun.
- Team members appreciate not having to adjust to a random new manager - Ray is the new interim manager.
- Belal and team member had happy hour in NYC and co-working session which was very enjoyable.
- Emily, Brandyn, Ray, and Abhinav hosted a team member in Seattle for a week.

#### Technical Achievements
- **Proxima Work Success** - Huge shoutout to Brandyn, Kinsey and others for their work on proxima and testing CCA surface areas.

- Ray drove the Job queuing work with Colby, ensuring readiness for GA with amazing progress.

#### Operational Improvements
- **On-call is improving** - Multiple mentions that on-call feels better compared to a month ago and less like a nightmare.
- Only got paged once during the period (and it was transient).
- On-call rotation is less frequent with new folks joining.
- On-call has seemed to calm down some.

#### Project & Work Excitement
- Team member is excited to be DRI on sunsetting extensions and enjoys breaking down tasks.
- Excited about Ray stepping in as interim manager.
- Nice to hear about different vacations everyone is taking, including cool biking tours.
- The agent-session-experience team is developing with more structure for surrounding teams.

### 2. Lacked/Do Less (Negatives & Areas for Improvement)

#### Communication & Coordination Challenges
- **Lack of clarity on DRI** - Still unclear who owns what, especially with external teams.
- Lots of DM interrupts - one team member mentioned needing to ignore people more.
- There's definitely a lot of slack noise.
- Hard to keep up with discussions in Slack but feeling like missing out on important conversations.
- Team member keeps getting tagged to fix blackbird issues / MCP in general.
- No clarity on who is in the coding-agent-team channel and their relevance.

#### Process & Planning Issues
- **Support escalations** - Question about whether Tim is handling these alone or if team should help as First Responders.
- Burned a candle at both ends for Proxima stuff for over a month - epic wasn't organized well.
- Universe launches are still not solidified.
- Need to hold ourselves accountable to solve FR issues and not just feature work, but FR work feels like it gets fewer "points".

#### Workload & Focus
- Feels like the sessions experience team is getting a lot of the shiny new toy attention.

- Need to refine FR backlog.
- Need high-level overview of what's coming in larger group meetings.
- Try out tools to summarize Slack.
- Whole org needs to run and communicate more clearly.

#### Support & Escalation
- There were automation suggestions like GitHub actions that create FR items or Slack notifications.
- Previously had something that automatically added first responder label on copilot-extensibility repo with a board for labeled PRs and Issues.

- **When will our offsite be?** - Answer: October, but maybe not just before Universe. Basically TBD but probably after Universe.
- **Universe Launch** - Tim is working to set this in stone, should be next week or two.
- **GA Load** - Curious if team has had a voice at the table about going to GA and what additional load is expected.

- Already pairing and doing brownbags - continue this.

- Be kind to yourself with timelines/estimates - spend time in beginning to get estimate to help avoid over-committing.

#### Context & Notes
