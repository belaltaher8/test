## 💡 Learnings, Questions & Concerns

- FR issues may be deprioritized in favor of feature work, which leads to accumulating debt
- `sessions-experience` team appears to be getting more attention/"shiny new toy" treatment
- Potential for tooling (GitHub Actions, Slack AI summarization) to reduce noise and improve async communication
- Discussion around whether Slack AI recap tools could help with information overload
- Organizational framing matters: "Belal is *The MCP Guy*" vs. "Belal is on a team"

**Action Items Identified:**
- Refine the FR backlog
- Provide high-level overviews in larger group meetings
- Explore tools to summarize Slack
- Route interrupts to public team channels and create issues instead
- Assign Copilot to write docs on our code
- Continue pairing and brownbags

---

## 🚀 Take Aways

- Universe release documentation should be searchable (e.g., "universe release" in GitHub/releases)
- Tim is working to finalize the offsite date — likely October, after Universe
- Be kind to yourself with timelines and estimates — invest time upfront to avoid over-committing
- Explore GitHub Actions or Slack bots to automate FR issue creation from Slack messages
- Look into Slack AI summarization methodology
