## Executive Summary

This retrospective board captures feedback from the Copilot Extensibility Team following a sprint focused on Proxima work, onboarding new team member Dan, and operational improvements. The team celebrated significant improvements in on-call experience, successful collaboration on major initiatives, and welcoming Ray as interim manager. - **DM Interrupts:** High volume of direct message interruptions

- **Suggestion:** Use Slack AI for summarized recaps

- **Progress on Vision:** Team has made improvements with planning/release issues/demo meetings, but questioning if everyone feels the same
- **DRI Ownership:** Still lack of clarity on who owns what, especially with external teams
- **Universe Launches:** Launch timelines still not solidified
