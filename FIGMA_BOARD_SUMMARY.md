### What is a Retrospective Board?

Retrospective boards are typically used by agile teams to:
- Reflect on what went well during the sprint/period
- Identify what didn't go well or could be improved
- Discuss action items for future improvements
- Celebrate wins and acknowledge challenges

### Typical Retrospective Board Sections

Standard retrospective boards often include:
1. **What Went Well** - Positive aspects and successes
2. **What Didn't Go Well** - Challenges, blockers, or issues encountered
3. **Action Items** - Concrete steps to improve in the next iteration
4. **Kudos/Shoutouts** - Recognition for team members
5. **Questions/Discussion Topics** - Open items for team discussion
