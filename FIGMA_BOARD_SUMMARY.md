## Overall Team Sentiment

The retrospective reveals a team with **strong positive momentum** and cohesion:

- Improvements in on-call experience
- Strong collaboration and pairing
- Proactive about addressing challenges

**Key Challenges Being Addressed:**

- Balancing FR work with feature development
- Avoiding burnout through better planning and estimation

The team is actively working on concrete solutions to address these concerns, demonstrating a mature and self-aware engineering culture.
